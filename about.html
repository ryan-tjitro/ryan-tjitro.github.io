About Us

The Big Bread Theory is a blog dedicated to understanding the science behind all forms of baking and desserts--from cupcakes to ethnic deserts. Our goal is to help people understand what the ingredients in a recipe do for the final product.

This website began with a science geek with a passion for baking who sought for a unique website idea for a project in one of his classes. Today, The Big Bread Theory is a work in progress.
